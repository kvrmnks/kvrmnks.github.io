定义顶点集合为$N = \{(x,0), (0,x) | \forall x \in S\}$

边集$E = \{((a_{i},0), (a_{i+1},0)),((0,a_{i}), (a_{i+1},0)),((0,a_{i}), (0,a_{i+1})),((a_{i},0), (0,a_{i+1}))| 1 \leq i < |S|\}$

定义$\large V = (\frac{\sum w(a_{i})}{2}, \frac{\sum w(a_{i})}{2})$
